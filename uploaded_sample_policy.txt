SUNSHINE INSURANCE - HOME AND CONTENTS POLICY
Policy Number: SUN-2024-HOME
Effective Date: 1 January 2024

WHAT THIS POLICY COVERS
This policy covers your home and contents against loss or damage caused by fire, storm, flood, theft, and accidental damage. Coverage includes the main dwelling, attached structures such as garages, and all personal contents within the home up to the insured value.

Fire and Storm Coverage includes damage from bushfire, lightning, hail, and wind. We will pay to repair or rebuild your home to its original condition.

Theft and Burglary Coverage applies when there is evidence of forced entry. We cover stolen contents up to $50,000 and will replace items at current market value.

Flood Coverage applies to damage caused by riverine flooding and flash flooding. You must notify us within 7 days of the flood event.

Accidental Damage Coverage applies to sudden and unexpected damage to your home or contents caused by you or a family member.

WHAT IS NOT COVERED
The following situations are excluded from this policy. We will not pay claims for damage caused by gradual deterioration, wear and tear, or lack of maintenance. Damage from pests including termites, rodents, and insects is excluded. 

Intentional damage caused by you or anyone living at the property is not covered. Damage occurring while the property is left unoccupied for more than 60 consecutive days is excluded unless you have notified us in advance.

Pre-existing damage that existed before the policy start date is not covered. Mechanical or electrical breakdown of appliances is excluded.

HOW TO MAKE A CLAIM
To make a claim contact our claims team on 1800 SUNSHINE within 48 hours of the incident. You will need to provide your policy number, a description of what happened, photos of the damage, and a list of damaged or stolen items with estimated values.

A claims assessor will contact you within 2 business days to arrange an inspection if required. For emergency repairs you may proceed immediately and keep all receipts for reimbursement.

Claims are typically processed within 10 business days. Payment is made by direct bank transfer to your nominated account.

PREMIUM AND RENEWAL
Your annual premium is due on the policy anniversary date. We will send a renewal notice 30 days before expiry. If payment is not received within 14 days of the due date your policy will lapse and you will not be covered.

You may cancel this policy at any time by contacting us. A pro-rata refund will be provided for unused months.
